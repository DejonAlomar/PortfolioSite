About Alomar

I'm a freelance illustrator and character designer. I've been drawing for as long as I remember, being captivated by the diverse and colorful designs of videogame worlds and work to evoke the same sense of wonder i had growing playing them in my own art.

I love making characters most of all! I've designed hundreds of characters, both for myself and also for other people. I've included the ones I'm most proud of in my portfolio. If you've got an idea about a character, you better believe I can bring that idea to life! And if you're interested, take a look through my contacts and get in touch with me!
